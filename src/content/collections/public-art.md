Art does not only exist inside museums. Throughout Yerevan, sculpture, mosaics, fountains and monuments create an open-air gallery that accompanies everyday life.

Some works celebrate political events, others commemorate individuals or simply enrich public space. Together they reveal changing artistic styles, shifting ideologies and evolving ideas about the relationship between art and society.

Looking carefully transforms an ordinary walk into a visual conversation with the city. Many remarkable works remain unnoticed simply because they have become part of the everyday landscape.

This collection encourages visitors to slow down, observe closely and discover how public art continues to shape the character of Yerevan.
